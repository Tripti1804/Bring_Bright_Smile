Spread Smile

Under "Spread Smile" campaign, we first collect the things our locals don't need like books, clothes, toys, etc.. and then donate it to the needy ones like the poor who can't afford to buy stuffs. We know even the slightest efforts is a lot for them. We also make suitable food and then feed the underprivileged children. We are happy to serve the least to the most we can.
